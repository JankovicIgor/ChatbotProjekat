Payment Model & Discounts — essentials (per official tuition page)
• 12 monthly instalments WITHOUT interest (OAS/MAS).
• Upfront deposit: 20% at enrolment; if enrolling after Sept 1, pay 40% (deposit + 1st rate) immediately.
• Early‑bird: 10% discount for those who pay the 20% deposit by March 31, 2025 (first year only).
• Niš Centre discount: lower tuition applies for regular attendance; irregular attendance (>5 absences per course per semester) voids the discount.
Source: https://www.metropolitan.ac.rs/upis/skolarine/
